About us

Family Deserves
an Extraordinary Gifts

At OSERVCO, we aim to be an extension of your enterprise, assisting with the procurement of office consumables and customized business gifts.

Powered by a network of over 200 vendor partners and more than 1,000 products across various categories and brands, OSERVCO serves as your one-stop shop for all office consumable needs.

With a team that brings over 30 years of expertise in enterprise customer engagement and supply chain management, OSERVCO is well-equipped to meet and exceed market demands.
